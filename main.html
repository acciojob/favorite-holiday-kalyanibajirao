Maldives


The Maldives, a tropical paradise of pristine beaches, is an archipelago of 1,192 coral islands grouped into 26 coral atolls in the Indian Ocean. It's known for its stunning white-sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life.

  1. Maafushi Island: Experience local culture and affordable beachfront hotels.
  2. Banana Reef: Renowned for its fantastic diving spots and rich marine biodiversity.
  3. Male: The bustling capital city with historical landmarks and local markets.
  4. Baros Island: A luxurious escape with exclusive resorts and private beaches.
  5. Mirih Island: Known for its bioluminescent beaches and serene atmosphere.
  • Garudhiya: A fragrant fish soup that's a staple of Maldivian cuisine.
  • Mas Huni: A traditional breakfast dish made with tuna, onion, coconut, and chili.
  • Bis Keemiya: A savory samosa-like snack filled with eggs, onions, and cabbage.
  • Fihunu Mas: Grilled fish that's been marinated with a blend of Maldivian spices.
  • Rihaakuru: A thick tuna-based paste that's an essential component of many dishes.
